Vision

To achieve excellence in teaching, learning and research to develop quality engineers to meet the current trends in the emerging world of Information Technology.

Mission

Department constantly aims at providing quality technical education and works in pace with the modern scientific and technological development to meet the need of industry, business, the service sector and the society at large.

Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering. It's my pleasure to introduce the Department of Computer Engineering (Diploma) of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere. Since its establishment in 1995, Department of Computer Engineering has blessed with full of energetic, well-qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty and staff. With the need of Outcome Based Education, we have realized the importance of quality of education through systematic planning and execution but not by chance. To achieve our educational objectives, we organize, expert lectures on upcoming technologies, workshops to help them acquire core technology skills, industrial visits to sensitize students to the practical challenges that organizations face in the technical world. In order to make students to environtal and socially sensitive, economically and culturally aware, department organizes various extracurricular and co-curricular activities. We believe in inclusive teaching learning process to make skilled and all-round engineers. Active participation of the student’s organization ‘OCEAN’, in all these event organization, helps them to develop organizational capabilities. Department strives to keep state-of-the-art laboratory and classroom facilities. Taking into account the technological advancement and the information explosion, we take sincere efforts to make them good professionals and sensitive citizens. Our enthusiastic, supportive and loving alumnus helps us a lot in the achievement of our goals.
